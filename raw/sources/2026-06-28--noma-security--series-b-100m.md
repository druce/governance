## Key extracted facts
- Founded 2023 by Niv Braun (CEO) and Alon Tron; emerged from stealth November 2024.
- HQ: Tel Aviv (product/R&D); New York (operations/US).
- Series B: $100M, July 2025, led by Evolution Equity Partners; participation from Ballistic Ventures, Glilot Capital, Cyber Club London, Databricks Ventures, SVCI.
- Series A: Oct 2024, led by Ballistic Ventures.
- Total funding ~$132M across rounds (Crunchbase/Tracxn).
- ARR up 1,300%+ in the past year.
- Product: unified AI + agent security platform — continuous AI discovery, AI security posture management (AI-SPM), risk prioritization, AI red teaming, runtime protection for AI agents. Deploys across cloud, code repositories, and AI development platforms.
- Customers: Fortune 500 across financial services, life sciences, retail, technology.
- Self-positioning (marketing): "the only cybersecurity vendor to offer an end-to-end platform purpose-built to identify and mitigate the uniquely advanced risks of agentic AI."
