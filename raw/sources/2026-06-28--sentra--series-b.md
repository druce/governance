## Key facts
- Founded 2021. HQ: Tel Aviv, Israel; offices in New York City.
- Founders: Yoav Regev (CEO, ex-Head of Cyber Dept, Unit 8200), Asaf Kochan (President, former Commander of Unit 8200), Ron Reiter (CTO), Yair Cohen (VP Product, ex-Microsoft/Datadog).
- What it does: cloud-native, agentless DSPM. Discovers and classifies sensitive data across cloud (uses "Data Security Posture Management" + data detection & response). Adds controls to keep AI training sets and gen-AI prompts free of PII/restricted data. Markets data access governance and an AI data security / "data + AI" angle.
- Funding: $50M Series B (Apr 2025), led by Key1 Capital; existing investors Bessemer Venture Partners, Zeev Ventures, Standard Investments, Munich Re Ventures. Brings total funding to over $100M. Prior: $30M Series A (raised total to $53M at that time).
- M&A: independent; no acquisition. No seed M&A flag.
- Target customer: cloud-heavy enterprises adopting AI.
