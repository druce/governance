## Key extracted facts
- Google Security Operations (Google SecOps), formerly Chronicle Security Operations; rebranded from Chronicle to Google Security Operations in April 2024.
- Owned by Google (Alphabet / Google Cloud). Public parent: Alphabet (NASDAQ: GOOGL/GOOG).
- Chronicle origin: started as a project within Alphabet's X moonshot lab; became Google Cloud product.
- Mandiant acquisition: Google announced March 2022 ($23.00/share, all-cash, ~$5.4B); completed Sep 12 2022. Added frontline threat intel, IR, MDR, consulting.
- Gemini AI: agentic capabilities central. Alert Triage & Investigation agent and Threat Intel agent entered public preview late 2025, GA 1Q26. AI-generated case summaries, response recommendations, context-aware chat, NL detection/playbook creation.
- Named a Leader in IDC MarketScape Worldwide SIEM 2026; elevated to Leader in Gartner MQ for SIEM 2025.
- Petabyte-scale ingestion noted by Gartner.
