GovernGPT product summary (vendor marketing):

What it does: Automates DDQ responses for investment managers. AI drafts answers by
drawing from a firm's existing documents and approved content libraries; reduces manual
work and accelerates LP response cycles.

Core features:
- AI-powered drafting "in your firm's voice" learning from historical DDQ answers,
  pitchbooks, internal documents.
- Content library management — central repository of approved/compliance-reviewed language.
- Automated workflow routing — questions route to subject-matter experts (compliance,
  IT, investments) for review/approval.
- Audit trails & version control — "Audit trails log every edit and approval, giving
  your compliance team a clear record" (positioned for SEC examinations, LP follow-ups).
- Multi-fund support — separate data structures per fund vintage/strategy.
- Document ingestion — "automatically ingests documents without manual tagging."

Deployment: not specified (cloud vs on-prem). Integrates with document and CRM systems
managers already run. No hosting location / data residency / certifications stated.

Note: "compliance" here = the manager's own regulatory disclosures (SEC/FINRA marketing
review, LP DDQs), NOT AI-model governance / SR 11-7 model risk.
