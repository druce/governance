- Product: "Data Access Platform" / data security platform. Attribute-based access control (ABAC) said to "reduce the number of policies required by 75x." Data discovery & classification, sensitive data tagging, dynamic policy enforcement, masking, monitoring/audit, and (newer) data security posture / detection.
- Deployment: SaaS ("Immuta SaaS in minutes"); native integrations with Snowflake, Databricks, Amazon (Redshift), Google (BigQuery), Azure, Starburst — pushes down policy into the cloud data platform rather than proxying.
