## CyberArk
- Founded 1999 in Israel by Udi Mokady and Alon N. Cohen. HQ Petah Tikva, Israel (US ops Newton, MA).
- Public: NASDAQ: CYBR (IPO 2014) until acquired by Palo Alto Networks (closed 2026-02-11).
- Core: Privileged Access Management (PAM); expanded into secrets management, identity governance, non-human/machine identity, workforce/customer identity.

## Conjur
- CyberArk acquired Conjur Inc. (Newton/Waltham, MA) in May 2017 for ~$42M.
- Conjur = secrets management for DevOps / cloud-native pipelines; CyberArk later released Conjur Open Source (OSS) plus Conjur Enterprise / Secrets Manager.
- Conjur is a product line within CyberArk, not an independent company; now part of PANW via the CyberArk acquisition.
