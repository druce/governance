- Announced 2023-11-14; generally available early December 2023.

- Helps businesses and governments "govern and build trust in generative AI."
- Core: manage AI to meet upcoming safety and transparency regulations and policies worldwide; proactively identify and mitigate risk by monitoring for fairness, bias, drift, and new LLM metrics.
- Manages, monitors, and governs AI models from IBM, open-source communities, and other providers.
- Provides a "nutrition label" for AI transparency (factsheets concept).
- Quote: "Watsonx.governance is a one-stop-shop for businesses that are struggling to deploy and manage both LLM" and standard ML models.
- Target customers: businesses and government entities struggling to deploy and manage both large language models and traditional ML models.
- Addresses risks from unvalidated training data and lack of explainable outputs in generative AI.
