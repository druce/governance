Good for: Oso funding, founders, founding year, product (Polar, Oso Cloud), "Oso for Agents" pivot.

- Series A: $8.2M led by Sequoia (Mar 17 2021); SV Angel, Company Ventures, Highland Capital + angels (Dev Ittycheria/MongoDB, Calvin French-Owen/Segment, Charity Majors/Honeycomb, Edith Harbaugh/LaunchDarkly). Total raised ~$10.9M (incl. 2019 Sequoia seed); other sources cite up to ~$26M cumulatively.
- Founded: ~2018 by Graham Neray and Sam Scott. Fully remote; New York, NY base.
- Product: Polar — declarative policy language (built in Rust) for RBAC/ReBAC/ABAC. Oso Library (embeddable, multi-language) — now deprecated/legacy. Oso Cloud — managed authorization service; claims 1M+ req/s, <10ms latency.
- 2025 pivot: "Oso for Agents" — AI agent security & control platform. Tagline: "Your employees ignore 96% of their permissions. Agents won't." Discover agents (laptop/browser/terminal), monitor every prompt/tool-call/response, detect rule violations / PII / credential leakage, control via policy ("block unknown MCP servers," "deny all delete"), report/audit.
