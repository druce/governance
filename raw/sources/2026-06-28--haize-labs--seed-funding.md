## Extracted

Haize Labs = AI safety / automated red-teaming startup; stress-tests LLMs at scale
("haizing") to find failure modes, jailbreaks, vulnerabilities, then hardens them.
Also builds j1 / judge models and evaluation tooling ("Deploy 99.9% Reliable AI").

- Founded 2023. Founders: Leonard Tang (CEO, 22 y/o), Richard Liu, Steve Li — met as
  Harvard undergrads; Tang & Li were Berkeley AI Research (BAIR) undergrad researchers.
- HQ: New York, NY (222 Broadway).
- Seed: $12.5M led by General Catalyst at ~$100M valuation, August 2024 (~8 months
  after founding); investors reportedly competed on price. Other angels: founders of
  Okta, Hugging Face; Amjad Masad (Replit), Scott Wu (Cognition), Demi Guo (Pika),
  Neil Shen (Sequoia China); Soma Capital, OVO Fund.
- Customers: Anthropic named as a customer.
- Deployment: SaaS / API; red-team suite + judge models.
- Ownership: independent, VC-backed.
