- Announced 2025-11-19: Oasis Agentic Access Management (AAM), "the first identity solution purpose-built to govern AI agents throughout their entire lifecycle." Operates at the identity/access layer for autonomous AI agents across enterprise systems.
- Capabilities:
  1. Intent Inference — LLM-driven interpretation of agent objectives so policies evaluate purpose, not just actions.
  2. Deterministic Policy Enforcement — policy engine validating access decisions; escalates with human oversight when privilege boundaries crossed.
  3. Just-in-Time Session Identities — ephemeral, least-privilege credentials for short sessions; eliminates standing secrets; full audit trails.
- HQ: New York. Founded: 2022. Co-Founders: Danny Brickman (CEO) and Amit Zimerman.
- Brickman: "Agentic Access Management is the next evolution of our platform: bringing structure, accountability, and trust to AI agents without slowing their impact."
- AAM extends the Oasis non-human identity management platform with specialized governance for autonomous AI agents at scale.
