Key extracted text:
- Founded 2006 by Mike Walters and Alex Vovk. First product (2007): Active Directory change auditing software.
- Acquisitions: merged with Stealthbits Technologies (data security / PAM) on 2021-01-04; acquired PolicyPak (Windows desktop management) Oct 2021; acquired Usercube (France-based identity governance & administration / IGA) Aug 2022.
- Additional acquisitions noted: Strongpoint, ANIXIS, New Net Technologies (NNT), among others.
- Geographic expansion across US, UK, Germany, France, Latin America, Asia.
