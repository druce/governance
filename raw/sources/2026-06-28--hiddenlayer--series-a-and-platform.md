## Key extracted facts
- Founded **March 2022**; emerged from stealth July 2022. HQ **Austin, Texas**. CEO/co-founder **Chris "Tito" Sestito**.
- **$50M Series A** announced **2023-09-19**, led by **M12 (Microsoft's Venture Fund)** and **Moore Strategic Ventures**, with Booz Allen Ventures, IBM Ventures, Capital One Ventures, Ten Eleven Ventures. Largest Series A for an AI-security company in 2023 (marketing claim).
- Ownership: **independent** (no acquisition found as of 2026-06-28). ~169 employees as of 2026-03-31 (per Tracxn via search).
- Platform (AISec Platform 2.0, unveiled April 2025) covers four areas:
  - **AI discovery / Model Scanning** — detect malicious models, backdoored weights, vulnerable dependencies before deployment.
  - **Supply chain security.**
  - **Runtime defense (MLDR — AI Detection & Response)** — non-invasive, software-based monitoring of AI inputs/outputs, real-time defense + response.
  - **Attack simulation (red teaming)** — continuous adversarial testing.
- Known for AI/ML model security research (e.g., model file format exploits, "ShadowLogic", Knight Capital-style attack research) and a model scanner.
