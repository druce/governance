# Clutch Security raises $20M Series A — vendor blog + press

URL: https://www.clutch.security/blog/clutch-security-20M-series-a
Fetched: 2026-06-28
Good for: Funding, founders, product scope (NHI security platform), target customers. Vendor blog — corroborated by TechCrunch/SecurityWeek/Calcalist.

## Key extracted text

Funding: $20M Series A led by SignalFire; participation from Lightspeed Venture Partners and
Merlin Ventures. Total funding $28.5M. Announced January 30, 2025.

Founder/CEO: Ofir Har-Chen (co-founders Sagi Haas and Tal Kimhi per Calcalist). 15+ yrs
cybersecurity. Founded October 2023. HQ Tel Aviv, Israel.

Product: Non-human identity security platform. Secures API keys, secrets, service accounts,
tokens, and certificates powering cloud infrastructure, CI/CD pipelines, and AI systems.
Capabilities: discovery/inventory of NHIs; lifecycle management and posture assessment;
Zero Trust verification for NHIs; threat detection and response; AI agent guardrails and
secret governance; shadow AI discovery.

Philosophy: replaces traditional secret rotation with real-time dynamic controls and
ephemeral identities ("Zero Trust for NHIs").

Target: banking, financial services, insurance, technology sectors.

Deployment: connects "to wherever your Identities, Agents, and Secrets live" — cloud-agnostic.
