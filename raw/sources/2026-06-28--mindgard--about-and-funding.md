## Extracted

Mindgard provides automated AI red teaming and security testing. Helps organizations
"discover, assess, and defend their AI systems" — red teaming, vulnerability
identification, and runtime protection across AI models, agents, and applications.

- Founded 2022; spinout of AI-security research by Dr. Peter Garraghan at Lancaster
  University ("traditional AppSec could not address AI-specific risks").
- Founders: Dr. Peter Garraghan (Chief Science Officer & Founder), Dr. Neeraj Suri
  (CSO), Steve Street (CRO/COO). James Brear hired as CEO (2025). Aaron Portnoy = CPO.
- HQ: Boston and London.
- Funding: $11.6M+ total. $8M round Dec 2024 led by .406 Ventures, with Atlantic Bridge,
  Willowtree Investments; earlier investors IQ Capital, Lakestar.
- Positioning: "leader in AI Red Teaming and Security Testing." Offensive AI security.
- Target customers: enterprise incl. Fortune 500 design partners.
- Deployment: SaaS platform (book a demo); details of API/CLI not on About page.
- Ownership: independent, VC-backed.
