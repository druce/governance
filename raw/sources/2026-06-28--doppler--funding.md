## Key extracted facts
- Product: centralized, cloud-based (SaaS) secrets management platform — "SecretOps." Stores app config + credentials in one place, syncs them to apps, CI/CD, cloud, and dev environments; access control, audit logs, versioning, rotation, integrations with AWS/GCP/Azure/Vercel/K8s etc.
- Founded: 2018. HQ: San Francisco, CA.
- Funding: ~$28.9M total. Seed ~$2.3M (Sequoia, incl. Peter Thiel). Series A $20M (2022, led by CRV / per TechCrunch). Y Combinator alum. Independent, VC-backed.
- Positioning: developer-first SaaS secrets manager (vs self-hosted HashiCorp Vault / cloud-native KMS). Nearest neighbor: Infisical (open-source challenger).
