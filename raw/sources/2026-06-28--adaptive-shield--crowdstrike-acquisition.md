## Key extracted facts

- CrowdStrike announced definitive agreement to acquire Adaptive Shield on 2024-11-06, at Fal.Con Europe (Amsterdam).
- Reported deal value ~$300M (SecurityWeek; Calcalist). Press release: "predominantly in cash" with a stock component subject to vesting; amount not officially disclosed.
- Expected to close in CrowdStrike fiscal Q4 (i.e., by ~end of January 2025).
- Adaptive Shield is a SaaS Security Posture Management (SSPM) leader. Capabilities: visibility/governance over misconfigurations, entitlements, and activity across 150+ SaaS apps for human and non-human identities; SaaS identity threat detection (ITDR); and GenAI application security — monitoring AI SaaS apps, detecting config drift, and identifying shadow AI.
- George Kurtz (CrowdStrike CEO): "With the acquisition of Adaptive Shield, CrowdStrike will continue to set the standard for identity-based protection in the cloud, delivering best-in-class SaaS protection from the Falcon platform."
- Maor Bin (Adaptive Shield CEO): "Our mission perfectly complements CrowdStrike, stopping SaaS breaches..."

## Adaptive Shield background
- Founded 2019. HQ Tel Aviv, Israel.
- CEO/co-founder Maor Bin.
- Raised ~$44M from Insight Partners, Okta Ventures, Vertex Ventures Israel, Blackstone Growth.
- Now CrowdStrike's SSPM offering, integrated into Falcon.
