Good for: Weaviate ownership (independent), funding, founding, HQ.

Weaviate (company formerly SeMI Technologies) builds the open-source Weaviate
vector database plus a managed cloud.
- Founded: 2019 by Bob van Luijt (creator). HQ: Amsterdam, Netherlands (with US presence).
- Series B: $50M led by Index Ventures with NEA, Battery, ING Ventures (April 2023).
- Series C: ~$50M led by Battery Ventures and Index Ventures, with NEA (Oct 2025),
  reported ~$200M valuation. Total raised ~$118M (reports vary $67–118M).
- Product: open-source (BSD-3) vector database + Weaviate Cloud (managed SaaS);
  self-hostable. Still independent as of 2026-06.
