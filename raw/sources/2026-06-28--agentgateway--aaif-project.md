agentgateway is hosted by the Agentic AI Foundation (AAIF), a Linux Foundation project —
described as AAIF's newest hosted project, focused on agentic AI infrastructure.

Solo.io created agentgateway plus related OSS projects (kagent, agentregistry, agentevals),
using it internally to improve operational efficiency with agentic AI at Solo.io.

Governance: AAIF has a Governing Board, Technical Committee, and Working Groups structure.

Purpose: "Unified gateway for agentic AI, MCP, and services" — apply consistent security,
observability, routing, and governance across AI, agent, and application traffic.

Status: open-source and operational; active blog and GitHub presence.

Note: After the Aug 2025 Linux Foundation donation, the project landed under AAIF (the
Agentic AI Foundation) as its longer-term, agent-infra-aligned home within the LF.
