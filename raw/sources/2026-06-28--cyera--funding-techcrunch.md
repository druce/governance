## Key facts
- Founded 2021 by Yotam Segev (CEO) and Tamar Bar-Ilan (CTO); both ex-Israeli Unit 8200.
- HQ: New York (US); strong Israel R&D presence.
- What it does: DSPM — maps where sensitive data lives across cloud systems and databases, tracks how employees and apps use it, identifies vulnerabilities. Converged DSPM + DLP + identity into one AI-native platform. Secures data/AI for ~20% of the Fortune 500 (financial services, retail, media, healthcare).
- Latest confirmed round: $400M Series F at $9B valuation, 2026-01-08. Lead: Blackstone. Others: Accel, Coatue, Cyberstarts, Georgian, Greenoaks, Lightspeed, Redpoint, Sapphire, Sequoia, Spark.
- Total funding raised: over $1.7 billion.
- Valuation trajectory: $1.4B (Series C, Apr 2024) → $3B (Series D, Nov 2024) → $6B (Series E, ~$540M, Jul 2025) → $9B (Series F, Jan 2026).
- Search results also referenced a reported ~$12B valuation by June 2026 — NOT independently confirmed from a primary source here; treat as unverified.
- M&A: independent, well-funded, no acquisition. (Cyera itself has acquired smaller firms, e.g. Trail Security DLP in 2024.)
