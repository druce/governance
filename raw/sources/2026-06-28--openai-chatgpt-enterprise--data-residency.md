Good for: data residency / regional storage + inference residency for ChatGPT Enterprise.

Key extracted facts:
- Data residency at rest available in: Europe, United Kingdom, United States, Canada, Japan, South Korea, Singapore, India, Australia, United Arab Emirates.
- Covered content: conversations, uploaded files, custom GPTs, image generation artifacts, and more (customer content at rest stored in-region).
- Eligible plans: API, ChatGPT Enterprise, and ChatGPT Edu.
- Cost: data residency included at no additional cost for ChatGPT Enterprise and Education plans.
- Inference residency: eligible customers can opt into in-region GPU inference in the U.S. or Europe; API customers may explicitly select U.S. or Europe for data processing on supported endpoints.
- Europe data residency introduced earlier (2025); subsequently expanded worldwide to the country list above.
