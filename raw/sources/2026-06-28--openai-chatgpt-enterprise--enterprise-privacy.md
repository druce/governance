Key extracted facts (as surfaced from openai.com primary pages via search):
- No training on business data: "By default, OpenAI's models are not trained using data from ChatGPT business plans or the API unless a customer explicitly opts in to share data." Applies to ChatGPT Enterprise, Team/Business, and API. Models do not learn from usage by default.
- Data ownership: ChatGPT Enterprise gives the customer ownership and control over business data (inputs and outputs).
- Encryption: AES-256 at rest; TLS 1.2+ in transit between customers and OpenAI.
- Certifications: SOC 2 Type 2 (API Platform and ChatGPT Enterprise audited/certified); ISO/IEC 27001, 27017, 27018, 27701; CSA STAR. Most recent SOC 2 report period cited Jan 1 2025 – Jun 30 2025.
- Compliance support: GDPR, CCPA; DPA available. Data Processing Addendum offered.
- Retention: admins/users control how long data is retained in ChatGPT Enterprise; deleted conversations removed within 30 days unless required for security/legal.
- Audit log: workspace admins can access an audit log of conversations and GPTs through the Enterprise Compliance API.
- Trust portal: trust.openai.com (SafeBase) hosts certifications, DPA, subprocessor list, security documentation.
