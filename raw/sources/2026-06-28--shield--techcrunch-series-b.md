TechCrunch — "Shield, a communication compliance platform for financial institutions,
raises $20M" (2022-12-01)

Funding:
- Series B: $20M, December 2022
- Series A: $15M, January 2022 (total ~$35M raised in 2022)
- Series B led by Macquarie Capital; co-investors UBS Next, Mindset Venture, OurCrowd

Founding & leadership:
- Founded ~2018 (described as "four-year-old startup")
- CEO & Co-founder: Shiran Weitzman

HQ: Tel Aviv and New York.

Platform: communication compliance & surveillance for financial institutions —
"record management, electronic discovery, supervision and surveillance"; monitors
comms across multiple channels for regulatory compliance.

AI: "leans on AI techniques to help companies counter market abuse, bad internal
actors and regulatory risk." Launched eDiscovery; partnered with Intelligent Voice
to enhance voice and NLP surveillance.

Target: banks, investment firms, private equity; SEC compliance and financial-crime
detection.

Growth (vendor-reported): sales +280% YoY; customers +250% since Series A.
