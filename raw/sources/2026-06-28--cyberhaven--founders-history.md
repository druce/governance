Key extracted text:
- Founded 2016 by Cristian Zamfir, Volodymyr Kuznetsov, George Candea, Radu Banabic, and Vitaly Chipounov in Lausanne, Switzerland. Founders are computer scientists / security researchers from EPFL (and MIT background cited).
- Initially established in Lausanne, Switzerland; later moved HQ to Palo Alto, California.
- Early 2024: launched Linea AI and its Large Lineage Model (LLiM). LLiM predicts the next likely action in corporate workflows (analogous to LLMs predicting next word).
- Core innovation: data lineage technology tracking data origin and movement, basis for the Data Detection and Response (DDR) platform.
- Leadership noted by profiles: Volodymyr Kuznetsov CTO; some profiles list Nishant Doshi as current CEO (Howard Ting listed as CEO in April 2025 press release — possible leadership transition; treat as unverified, soft).
