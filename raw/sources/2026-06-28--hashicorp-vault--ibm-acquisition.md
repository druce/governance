## Key extracted facts
- Price: $35.00/share cash; enterprise value $6.4 billion.
- Announced: 2024-04-24. Closed: 2025-02-27 (after ~10 months of regulatory review; FTC and UK CMA both reviewed and cleared).
- HashiCorp products: Terraform (IaC provisioning), **Vault** (identity-based secrets management / secrets + encryption), Boundary (secure remote access), Consul (service networking), Nomad (orchestration), Packer, Waypoint.
- HashiCorp HQ: San Francisco, CA. Founded 2012 by Mitchell Hashimoto and Armon Dadgar (per Wikipedia). Was publicly traded (Nasdaq: HCP) before the deal.
- Now a subsidiary of IBM. Vault continues as IBM's secrets-management offering.
