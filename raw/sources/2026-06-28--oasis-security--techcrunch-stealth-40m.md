- Oasis Security left stealth Jan 31, 2024 with $40M total ($5M seed + $35M Series A).
- Series A led by Sequoia Capital (Doug Leone, Bogomil Balkansky). Other investors: Accel, Cyberstarts, Maple Capital; angels Guy Podjarny (Snyk founder), Michael Fey (Island co-founder).
- Founders: Danny Brickman (CEO) and Amit Zimerman (Chief Product Officer). Both served in IDF cybersecurity; collaborated on a classified project that won an army innovation prize. Brickman spent 7+ years in IDF cyber.
- HQ described as Tel Aviv (early). [Later primary sources list HQ as New York, NY.]
- Founding ~2022-2023 (article says funding came "less than a year after founded"; later Oasis materials say founded 2022).
- Product: non-human identity management — "discover, resolve, automate". Maps all NHI interactions across networks; tracks data movement; identifies authentication anomalies; automated/manual remediation.
- Deployment: agentless, SaaS-based visualization + remediation platform.
- Early customers: Chipotle, JLL, Mercury Financial.
- Quote: "If you have 50 times more non-human identities than human ones, that means the attack surface is 50 times larger." — Danny Brickman
