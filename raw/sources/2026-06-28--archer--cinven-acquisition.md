Good for: Archer ownership chain — RSA spinout, now owned by Cinven (PE) since July 2023.

- Acquirer: Cinven (European private equity firm).
- Target: Archer — GRC / integrated risk management software, founded 2000, HQ Kansas.
  Serves >half of Fortune 500. (Formerly "RSA Archer".)
- Seller: RSA Security, a portfolio company of Clearlake Capital + Symphony Technology
  Group (STG). Announced 2023-04-13; sale completed 2023-07-10.
- Ownership chain: Archer was part of RSA, which Dell sold to STG in 2020; STG +
  Clearlake established RSA/Archer as independent (2021); Archer GRC carved out and
  sold to Cinven (2023). Deal value not disclosed.
- So: Archer is a standalone Cinven (PE) portfolio company, NOT "independent" in the
  founder/VC sense. ownership = acquired (PE-owned).
