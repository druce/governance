# Relativity Trace — product launch & capabilities

Good for: what Trace does, launch date (2018), proactive surveillance, AI/ML, data sources, use cases, built on Relativity platform.

Key text:
- Launched November 6, 2018. "An application built on the Relativity platform" that "helps businesses operating in highly regulated industries to proactively monitor internal communications and flag the highest-risk content for further review."
- Proactive communication surveillance: assesses data in near-real time and alerts compliance officers to suspicious content based on customized rules and parameters; leverages speed of search and machine learning to identify text/metadata of interest.
- Ingests communication data — email, audio, Slack/chat, text/short messages, and other configured channels — and applies predefined rules + ML to identify communications between actors involved in misconduct.
- Detects regulatory misconduct: insider trading, collusion, improper investment management practices, bribery, market abuse, other high-risk scenarios.
- Out-of-the-box surveillance policies offered (2020); AI-powered data-cleansing for short-message data (2022).
- Target industries: banking, investment management, financial services, pharmaceuticals.
- Delivery partners / managed-service offerings via Deloitte, FTI Consulting, Complete Discovery Source (CDS).
- In use across US and EMEA since 2018 launch.
