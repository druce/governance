## Key facts
- Founded 2018 by Karthik Krishnan (CEO).
- HQ: San Mateo, California.
- What it does: DSPM built on "Semantic Intelligence" — uses deep learning/NLP to autonomously categorize unstructured + structured data, assess risk, and remediate (e.g. fix oversharing, permissions) without upfront rules or regex configuration. Strong on unstructured data (documents, messages) and data access governance / oversharing remediation. Positions in "AI-driven Data Security Governance."
- Funding: $45M Series B (Oct 2024), led by Top Tier Capital Partners and HarbourVest Partners; existing investors Ballistic Ventures, Engineering Capital, Clear Ventures, Citi Ventures. Total funding over $67M.
- M&A: independent; no acquisition. No seed M&A flag.
- Target customer: mid-market to enterprise; emphasis on Microsoft 365 / unstructured data oversharing use cases.
