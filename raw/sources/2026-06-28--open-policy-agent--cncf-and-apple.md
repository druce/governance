Good for: OPA CNCF status, what OPA is, Rego, governance after Apple acqui-hire.

- OPA: open-source, general-purpose policy engine. Policies in Rego (declarative). First CNCF project focused entirely on authorization.
- CNCF timeline: accepted Mar 29 2018; Incubating Apr 2 2019; Graduated Jan 29 2021.
- Created by Styra (Tim Hinrichs, Torin Sandall, Teemu Koponen); donated to CNCF. License: Apache 2.0.
- Aug 2025: core maintainers acqui-hired by Apple (see Styra source). Project remains under CNCF governance; community continues stewardship; release cadence maintained.
- Use beyond infra/K8s: increasingly pitched as a guardrail for AI agents / MCP — define in code what agents/tools are allowed to do, enforce allow/deny fast and consistently.
