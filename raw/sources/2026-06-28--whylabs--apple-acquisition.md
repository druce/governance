## Key extracted facts
- M&A CONFIRMED (medium confidence — no official Apple announcement; Apple rarely confirms acquisitions): GeekWire reported in 2025 that WhyLabs founders joined Apple following an under-the-radar acquisition. Corroboration: AI Fund (Andrew Ng) portfolio page lists WhyLabs as "acquired in 2025"; former AI Fund GP Perry Wu lists WhyLabs on LinkedIn as "ACQ BY APPLE"; the deal surfaced via European Commission Digital Markets Act filings. Crunchbase records the acquisition (apple-acquires-whylabs).
- Effect on product: WhyLabs website now states the company is **discontinuing operations** and open-sourcing its platform (WhyLogs was already OSS). The standalone commercial WhyLabs AI Observatory / LangKit offering is effectively wound down.
- Founded 2019, incubated at Allen Institute for AI (AI2) by ex-Amazon ML engineers. HQ: Seattle.
- Founders/leaders: Alessya Visnjic (CEO, ex-Amazon), Andy Dang, Sam Gracie, Maria Karaivanova (ex-Cloudflare). Now at Apple in AI roles.
- Funding: ~$14M total over 2 rounds — $4M seed (Madrona) + $10M Series A (2021) co-led by Andrew Ng's AI Fund and Defy Partners, with Madrona and Bezos Expeditions (Jeff Bezos).
- Product (pre-acquisition): data & ML monitoring / AI observability at scale; WhyLogs OSS data-logging library; LangKit for LLM metrics; later added real-time GenAI security monitoring.
