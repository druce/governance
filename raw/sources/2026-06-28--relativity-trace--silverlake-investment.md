# Relativity Announces Strategic Investment from Silver Lake

URL: https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/relativity-announces-strategic-investment-from-silver-lake-301250508.html
Also: https://www.relativity.com/news-events/press-release-relativity-announces-strategic-investment-from-silver-lake/
fetched: 2026-06-28
Good for: Silver Lake ownership, $3.6B valuation, use of funds (RelativityOne + Trace), Andrew Sieja founder.

Key text:
- Investment date: March 18, 2021.
- Strategic growth investment from Silver Lake ("global leader in technology investing").
- Per WSJ reporting (corroborated by LawSites/Built In Chicago), deal valued Relativity at ~$3.6 billion; Silver Lake became largest shareholder with a significant minority stake; Silver Lake reps joined the board; management team unchanged.
- Prior investor ICONIQ Growth invested $125M in 2015 (when company was called kCura); remains a key investor. Founder Andrew Sieja remains a shareholder.
- Use of funds: "accelerate growth, innovation and technology differentiation" — expanding RelativityOne cloud globally, developing the AI-powered communication surveillance platform Relativity Trace, growing EMEA/APAC.
- Company: data management / e-discovery leader; 300,000+ annual users across 49 countries; serves legal, financial services, government; 198 of Am Law 200.
- Joe Osnoss (Silver Lake managing partner): "Relativity is not only the clear leader in software for the legal sector, but also an increasingly strategic company in enterprise technology generally."
- CEO Mike Gamson quote on the investment.
