## Key extracted facts
- **Acquisition announced 2026-06-15.** A10 Networks (NYSE: ATEN) acquired TrojAI Inc. Deal terms not disclosed. A10 expects no material impact on FY2026 results.
- Rationale: strengthen A10's "sovereign AI security" — pairing A10's hardware-based AI firewall with TrojAI's software-based red teaming + runtime protection across the AI lifecycle.
- TrojAI delivers two layers: **red teaming** (probes models, agents, apps for vulnerabilities at build time — TrojAI Detect) and **real-time threat protection** at runtime (TrojAI Defend).
- Quotes: Dhrupad Trivedi (A10 CEO): "AI is changing both what enterprises build and the attack surface they have to defend." Lee Weiner (TrojAI CEO): enterprises "need to innovate securely while maintaining sovereignty over their AI security infrastructure."

## Company background (from Crunchbase/BetaKit/NBIF/BigDATAwire via search)
- Founded 2019 (incorporated 2020) by **James Stewart** (CTO) and **Stephen Goddard**. CEO since 2024: **Lee Weiner** (ex-Rapid7).
- HQ: Saint John, New Brunswick, Canada; office in Boston.
- Funding pre-acquisition: ~$3M seed (early 2022); $5.75M USD seed April 2024 (Flying Fish Partners, Build Ventures, Techstars Ventures); NBIF investments. Total disclosed ~$9.65M USD.
