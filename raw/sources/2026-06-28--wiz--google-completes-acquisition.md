# Google completes $32B acquisition of Wiz

- URL: https://techcrunch.com/2026/03/11/google-completes-32b-acquisition-of-wiz/

## Key extracted facts
- Deal price: $32 billion, all cash. Largest acquisition in Google's history (exceeds 2012 Motorola Mobility $12.5B).
- Announced: March 2025. Closed: 2026-03-11 (~one year later).
- Regulatory: US DOJ clearance Oct 2025; EU + Australia Feb 2026; Singapore + Japan Mar 2026.
- Wiz joins Google Cloud but keeps its brand and continues to support all major clouds (AWS, Azure, GCP, Oracle). Plan to build a unified security platform.
- Wiz = cloud security platform (CNAPP) that prevents and responds to cloud threats; crossed $1B ARR in 2025.
- Google had initially offered ~$23B in 2024, which Wiz rejected.
- Wiz founded January 2020 (per Wikipedia) by Assaf Rappaport, Yinon Costica, Roy Reznik, Ami Luttwak (ex-Adallom/Microsoft). HQ New York City.
- Pre-acquisition funding (per Wikipedia/Crunchbase): ~$1.9B+ raised; Series E $1B in May 2024 at $12B valuation.
