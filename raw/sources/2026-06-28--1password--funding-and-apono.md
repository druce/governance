## Key extracted facts
- Company: 1Password, legal entity AgileBits Inc. Founded 2005. HQ: Toronto, Canada. Still independent (private, VC-backed).
- Funding: ~$920M total. Series C $620M at $6.8B valuation (Jan 2022), led by ICONIQ Growth (Tiger Global, Lightspeed, Accel + celebrity investors). Largest-ever round by a Canadian company at the time. 2024 revenue ~$318M ARR.
- Product: started as consumer password manager; pivoted to B2B "Extended Access Management" — securing credentials/passkeys/secrets + device trust for the "unmanaged" apps/devices accessing company resources. 100,000+ business customers (IBM, Slack, Shopify).
- M&A by 1Password: Kolide (endpoint/device trust, Feb 2024), Trelica (SaaS access mgmt/UK), Passage (passkeys, 2022).
- Apono acquisition: announced 2026-06-15. Apono = just-in-time / privileged access governance for humans, machines, and AI agents (dynamic, scoped, auto-revoked access). Reported $250M–$300M (Calcalist); other sources >$200M. Feeds "1Password Unified Access" + "1Password Credential Broker" (private beta, GitHub Actions workload identity first) — positioning 1Password as a control plane for human/machine/AI-agent access.
