## Key extracted facts
- Founded 2023; first-ever technology spinout from KPMG Studio (KPMG's startup incubator); incubated/funded in stealth inside KPMG.
- Founders: Jonathan Dambrot (CEO), Daniel Christman, Felix Knoll, Paul Spicer.
- HQ: Short Hills, New Jersey.
- Series A: $25M, announced Oct 26, 2023, led by Titanium Ventures; with KPMG LLP and SYN Ventures. Total funding ~$32M (seed + A).
- Product: enterprise AI security, governance & compliance — "map, monitor, and manage their AI/ML environments against adversarial threats"; AI risk assessment, compliance monitoring, AI Cards / supply-chain visibility, exposure management across GenAI.
- M&A (verified): On 2026-05-21 Cranium AI **acquired Aiceberg** (agentic AI security & risk management) to build an end-to-end AI security/governance/agentic platform. Cranium is the acquirer — it remains an independent company.
