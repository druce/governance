Key extracted text:

Third-party auditors assess the security and compliance of Amazon Q Business as part of multiple compliance programs. Amazon Q Business is compliant with:
- Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA)  [HIPAA-eligible since Oct 2024]
- System and Organization Controls (SOC) 1, 2, 3
- Payment Card Industry (PCI)
- International Standards Organization (ISO) 42001  [AI management system standard]

To learn whether an AWS service is in scope for a specific program, see AWS Services in Scope by Compliance Program. Download third-party audit reports via AWS Artifact.

Note: broader AWS (the platform) carries SOC 1/2/3, ISO 27001/27017/27018, FedRAMP, etc.; this page lists what Amazon Q Business specifically is attested for. FedRAMP availability for Q Business itself is via GovCloud (US) — verify scope per AWS Regional Services List.
