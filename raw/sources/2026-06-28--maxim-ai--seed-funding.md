## Extracted

Maxim AI = enterprise evaluation, simulation and observability platform for AI agents/
LLM apps; brings "test-driven development" to non-deterministic GenAI. Lifecycle from
design to production.

- Founded 2023 by Vaibhavi Gangwar (CEO, ex-Google Assistant NLP) and Akshay Deo
  (co-founder, ex-Postman). India + US presence; HQ commonly listed San Francisco /
  Mountain View, CA.
- Seed: $3M led by Elevation Capital, announced 18 June 2024; angels incl. founders of
  Postman, Chargebee, Groww, Razorpay, Media.net.
- Products: Experimentation (prompt CMS/IDE, workflow builder); Pre-release Evaluation
  Toolkit (machine + human eval, CI/CD); Observability Suite (prod monitoring, quality/
  safety); Data Engine (multimodal datasets). Also offers agent simulation and red-teaming.
- Deployment: SaaS; supports in-VPC deployments, RBAC, SDK/API.
- Claim (marketing): teams ship ~5x faster.
- Ownership: independent, VC-backed.
