## Key extracted facts
- Product: AI-native identity security & governance platform that continuously maps, monitors, and governs all identities across the enterprise — human, non-human, and AI agents.
- Founded: 2023, by Israel Duanis (CEO) and Niv Goldenberg.
- HQ: New York, NY.
- Total funding: $83M. Series B: $50M, announced 2026-03-31.
- Lead: Insight Partners; continued participation from Cyberstarts and Index Ventures.
- Target customers: banks, healthcare, Fortune 500 enterprises managing millions of identities.
- Recently introduced "Linx Autopilot," an autonomous AI agent for identity governance.
- Marketing framing: machine/service/AI agent identities outnumber humans ~80:1; "nearly 90% of security incidents involve identity-related failures."
