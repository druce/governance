## Key extracted facts
- Founded 2021; HQ Tel Aviv-Yafo, Israel.
- Founders: Ben Kliger (CEO, ex-Microsoft cloud workload protection product leader), Michael Bargury (CTO).
- Series B: $38M, Oct 29, 2024, co-led by Third Point Ventures and DTCP; with Microsoft's M12, Intel Capital, Vertex Ventures. Total raised over $55M.
- Product: end-to-end AI agent security & governance — visibility, risk assessment, governance for AI agents; secures enterprise copilots and low-code/no-code apps (research: 62% of copilots/low-code apps contain a security vulnerability). AI-SPM (discovery/inventory of agents, apps, automations) + agent runtime protection.
- Customers: Fortune 500 across financial services, technology, manufacturing, energy, pharma; large enterprises average ~80,000 AI agents/apps/automations.
- Known for AI agent red-teaming research (AgentFlayer / "Living off AI" prompt-injection work).
