## CrowdStrike (acquirer)
- Founded 2011 by George Kurtz, Dmitri Alperovitch, Gregg Marston.
- HQ Austin, Texas (relocated from Sunnyvale, CA).
- Public — NASDAQ: CRWD. IPO June 2019.
- Flagship: Falcon platform (EDR/XDR, cloud, identity, SIEM/Next-Gen SIEM via LogScale/Humio, Charlotte AI agentic SOC).
- AI-security M&A: Adaptive Shield (SSPM/GenAI; announced 2024-11-06, closed ~Jan 2025); Pangea (AI prompt-layer / AIDR; announced 2025-09-16, completed ~Sept 2025).
- Charlotte AI = internally built agentic SOC analyst (not an acquisition).
- LogScale (CrowdStrike Falcon LogScale) = from Humio acquisition (2021).

## SentinelOne (acquirer of Prompt Security)
- Founded 2013 by Tomer Weingarten (CEO), Almog Cohen, Ehud Shamir.
- HQ Mountain View, California.
- Public — NYSE: S. IPO June 2021.
- Flagship: Singularity platform (EDR/XDR, Purple AI agentic SOC, cloud, identity, data/SIEM via Scalyr).
- AI-security M&A: Prompt Security (GenAI runtime/DLP/shadow-AI; announced 2025-08-05, ~$250M, closed FY2026 Q3); also Observo AI (data pipeline, same announcement).
