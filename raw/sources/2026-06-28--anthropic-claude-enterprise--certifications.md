Good for: Anthropic's compliance certifications and which products they cover.

Anthropic maintains these compliance credentials (for commercial products):
- HIPAA-ready configuration (BAA available)
- ISO 27001:2022 (Information Security Management)
- ISO/IEC 42001:2023 (AI Management Systems)
- SOC 2 Type I & Type II

Scope statement (verbatim): "This article is about our commercial products such as Claude for Work and the Anthropic API." These certifications apply to commercial offerings, not consumer plans (Claude Free, Pro, Max).

Page references a Trust Portal (trust.anthropic.com, hosted on Vanta) for compliance documentation (SOC 2 report under NDA). GDPR/CCPA noted on enterprise marketing page rather than here.
