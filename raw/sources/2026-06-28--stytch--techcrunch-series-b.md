## Key extracted text

- Series B: $90M, November 2021, led by Coatue Management; valued Stytch at $1B (unicorn).

- HQ: San Francisco.
- CEO: Reed McGinley-Stempel (former Plaid).
- Product: API-first passwordless authentication — email/SMS magic links, OTPs, social sign-in (Apple/Google/Microsoft), WebAuthn hardware keys, biometrics.
- Target customer: developers building authentication into their apps.
- Note: founders/founding year not stated in this article. Other sources: founded 2020 in San Francisco by Reed McGinley-Stempel (CEO), Julianna Lamb (CTO), and Mark Cunningham.
