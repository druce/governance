## Key text
- Co-founded by Devin Redmond (CEO) and Rich Sutton (CTO). (Crunchbase/Tracxn also
  list Anthony J. Cresci as co-founder.)
- HQ: Santa Barbara, CA (233 E Canon Perdido St); additional office in New York.
- Self-describes as cloud- and AI-native platform for "Digital Communications
  Governance and Archiving (DCGA)" — capture, archive, detect, and surface risks
  across video, visual, voice, chat, document, email, and AI-generated content.
- Products: Unified Capture; Unified Search and Archiving; Proactive Compliance
  (ML/AI risk detection); AI Governance and Inspection Suite.
- Supported platforms: Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Webex by Cisco, RingCentral,
  Microsoft Exchange, Salesforce. Partnerships with Zoom AI Companion and Microsoft
  Copilot for AI interaction monitoring.
- AI/Copilot surveillance: monitors AI-generated content / AI tool interactions;
  specific integrations for Zoom AI Companion and Copilot.
- Ownership: venture-backed (Battery Ventures, Lightspeed, Cisco, Salesforce,
  Wells Fargo, RingCentral, Zoom, Neotribe and others). Independent / private.
- Named a Visionary in the 2025 Gartner Magic Quadrant for Digital Communications
  Governance and Archiving (DCGA).
- No Series C found as of 2026-06-28; latest disclosed round = Series B (2022).
