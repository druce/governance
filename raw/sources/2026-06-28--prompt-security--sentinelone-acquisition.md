## Key extracted facts

- SentinelOne announced definitive agreement to acquire Prompt Security on 2025-08-05.
- Deal: combination of cash and stock; amount not officially disclosed. Reported ~$250M (Calcalist).
- Expected to close in SentinelOne fiscal Q3 (FY2026), subject to regulatory approval. (SentinelOne later announced completion — "SentinelOne Completes Acquisition of Prompt Security.")
- Prompt Security: AI runtime security — enterprise visibility into GenAI tool usage; prevents data leakage, prompt injection, and unauthorized AI agent behavior; real-time protection across browsers, desktop apps, and APIs. Covers shadow-AI discovery, DLP for prompts, and AI runtime/guardrails.
- Tomer Weingarten (SentinelOne CEO): "AI is the most transformative force in the world today—but without security, it becomes a liability."
- Itamar Golan (Prompt Security CEO/co-founder): "SentinelOne shares our passion for empowering teams and organizations to embrace AI as a distinct advantage, while delivering real-time, automated protection."
