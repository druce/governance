Good for: Primary confirmation that IBM owns HashiCorp (and thus Terraform Cloud, HCP Terraform, Sentinel, Vault) — closing date + deal value.

IBM completed its acquisition of HashiCorp on February 27, 2025.
Deal value: $6.4 billion enterprise value ($35.00 per share in cash).

HashiCorp products now part of IBM include Terraform (infrastructure provisioning),
Vault (secrets management), and the broader HashiCorp portfolio (which includes the
Sentinel policy-as-code framework embedded in Terraform/Vault Enterprise & HCP).

Key quote: "HashiCorp has been a pioneer in cloud infrastructure since the earliest
days of public cloud" and will help IBM customers "automate, secure, and optimize
their cloud infrastructure." Terraform pairs with Red Hat Ansible; Vault complements
Red Hat OpenShift.

Corroborating: TechCrunch "IBM closes $6.4B HashiCorp acquisition" (2025-02-27);
SEC Form 8-K FY2025 (HashiCorp, Inc.).
