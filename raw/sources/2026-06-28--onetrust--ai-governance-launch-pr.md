## Key extracted text

OneTrust AI Governance helps organizations "inventory, assess, and monitor the wide range of risks associated with AI."

Capabilities:
- Inventory: "centrally view and manage AI projects, models, and datasets both internally developed and sourced from third parties."
- Risk Assessment: assess AI projects against NIST AI Risk Management Framework, OECD AI Principles, and the EU AI Act; automatically discovers and classifies sensitive data that may drive algorithmic bias.
- Monitoring: "integrates with existing model registries and MLOps tooling to detect AI model use and changes"; continuous evaluation for drift, bias, fairness.
- Compliance: supports conformity assessments and generates required compliance documentation aligned with ISO 42001.

Launch: introduced May 2023, initially via an Early Access Program (EAP) for select customers.
Target: compliance teams, data scientists, privacy teams.
