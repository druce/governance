## Extracted

Patronus AI = automated evaluation and security platform for generative AI; detects
LLM mistakes (hallucinations, accuracy, PII leakage, copyright, brand alignment) at
scale, "one line of code." Domain- and model-agnostic.

- Founded 2023 by ex-Meta AI researchers Anand Kannappan (CEO) and Rebecca Qian (CTO).
  Stealth launch Sept 2023.
- HQ: San Francisco, CA (some sources say NYC).
- Series A: $17M, June 2024, led by Notable Capital (formerly GGV Capital); with
  Lightspeed Venture Partners, Datadog, Gokul Rajaram, Factorial Capital. Brought total
  to ~$20M.
- Series B: $50M, 25 June 2026, led by Greenfield Partners; total ~$70M. With Notable,
  Lightspeed, Datadog, Samsung. Funds expanding into "Digital World Models" / large-scale
  simulation + reliability tools for autonomous AI systems.
- Products/research: Lynx (hallucination detection model), FinanceBench, CopyrightCatcher,
  EnterprisePII, Enterprise Scenarios Leaderboard, evaluator models / API (Glider, judges).
- Deployment: cloud/SaaS API; eval models. Serves Fortune 500 + AI companies.
- Ownership: independent, VC-backed.
