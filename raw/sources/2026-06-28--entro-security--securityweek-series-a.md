# Entro Security raises $18M Series A — SecurityWeek

## Key extracted text

Funding: $18M Series A led by Dell Technologies Capital. Total funding to date $24M
(incl. $6M seed in May 2023). (Series A announced June 2024.)

Founders: Adam Cheriki (CTO) and Itzik Alvas (CEO); both alumni of Israeli IDF cyber units.
HQ: Boston, Massachusetts (also operations in Tel Aviv).

Product: Non-human identity (NHI) and secrets management across multi-cloud and on-premises.
Automates the full lifecycle — discovery, vaulting, automatic rotation, decommissioning of
idle identities. Real-time detection and response across on-prem, cloud, and SaaS apps.
SOC2 and GDPR compliant. Agentless SaaS.

Target: organizations with thousands of unmanaged machine identities holding excessive
permissions.

Quote (CEO): "Both IBM and Verizon reports ranked NHI and secrets breaches as the second most
frequent attack and the costliest one."

Growth: expanding from 35 to ~80 employees by end of 2024.
