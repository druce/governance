# Palo Alto Networks Announces Agreement to Acquire CyberArk

URL: https://www.paloaltonetworks.com/company/press/2025/palo-alto-networks-announces-agreement-to-acquire-cyberark--the-identity-security-leader
Fetched: 2026-06-28
Good for: Primary confirmation of PANW–CyberArk deal terms, date, strategic rationale.

## Key facts
- Announcement date: July 30, 2025.
- Deal terms: $45.00 cash + 2.2005 PANW shares per CyberArk share; ~$25B equity value; 26% premium to 10-day avg VWAP as of 2025-07-25.
- Expected close: second half of PANW fiscal 2026, subject to regulatory + CyberArk shareholder approval.
- Strategic rationale: establishes Identity Security as a new core platform pillar; extend privileged identity controls across human, machine, and autonomous AI agents; just-in-time / least-privilege for agentic AI; integrate CyberArk into Strata and Cortex.
- Financials: immediately accretive to revenue growth and gross margin; accretive to FCF/share in FY2028.
- Quotes: CEO Nikesh Arora ("Every identity requires the right level of privilege controls, not the 'IAM fallacy'."); CyberArk founder Udi Mokady.

## Completion
- Per corroborating press (DelMorgan, GovConWire) and PANW: transaction CLOSED 2026-02-11 after shareholder approval and regulatory clearances (US, EU, UK, Israel).
- Corroborating SEC filings: PANW 8-K (d67702dex992), CyberArk 6-K (tm2522061d1), CyberArk Form 425 filings (2025).
