## Key extracted facts
- Founded 2021 in Paris, France by Alex Combessie and Jean-Marie John-Mathews (PhD), now co-CEOs; CTO Matteo Dora (PhD). HQ: Paris (Pantin).
- Independent (not acquired). Funding small / early-stage: ~$1.5–2M seed (reported ~Dec 2022), backers include Elaia, plus angels (CTOs/founders from Hugging Face, Mistral AI, Uber — e.g., Charles Gorintin). Sources disagree on exact total ($1.5M–$2M); no confirmed Series A.
- Non-dilutive: won a ~€3M Bpifrance / France 2030 grant (2024) to lead an LLM evaluation R&D consortium with Mistral AI, Artefact, INA, BnF.
- Product: open-source Python library (giskard-oss) for testing/evaluating ML & LLM models — quality, security, vulnerabilities; LLM vulnerability scanner (multi-turn, adversarial probes). Commercial **Giskard Hub** — enterprise platform for LLM agent testing, team collaboration, continuous red teaming. Positions on AI red teaming + LLM evaluation + EU AI Act compliance alignment.
- Customers: AXA, BNP Paribas, Michelin, Google DeepMind (per vendor site — marketing claim).
- Deployment: OSS library (SDK/self-hosted) + Giskard Hub (SaaS / self-managed enterprise).
