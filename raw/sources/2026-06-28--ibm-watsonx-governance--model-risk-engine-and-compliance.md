Key extracted facts:
- Model Risk Evaluation Engine: new tool in watsonx.governance developed with IBM Research; measures risks of foundation models by computing metrics tied to risk dimensions from the IBM AI Risk Atlas; enables comparison of risk metrics across foundation models to pick the most suitable for deployment.
- On install, watsonx.governance consolidates Watson OpenScale + AI factsheets + the Model Risk Governance capabilities of OpenPages into a single service.
  - OpenPages = central risk and compliance engine: translates company policies into trackable metrics per AI model; formal risk assessments; maps models to regulations; immutable audit trails for regulators.
  - OpenScale = continuous monitoring of accuracy, fairness, drift, plus model health (latency, throughput).
- Compliance accelerators preload ~12 regulatory frameworks, including EU AI Act, SR 11-7, NYC Local Law 144, ISO 42001, and NIST AI RMF.
- Automatically assesses each model against EU AI Act risk categories, flagging high-risk systems needing additional compliance measures.
- Vendor-agnostic: governs traditional and generative AI across IBM, AWS, Azure, OpenAI, and 50+ other platforms.
