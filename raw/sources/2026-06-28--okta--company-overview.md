Key facts:
- Okta, Inc. (Nasdaq: OKTA). Founded 2009 by Todd McKinnon and Frederic Kerrest (ex-Salesforce). HQ: 100 First Street, San Francisco, CA.
- IPO April 2017 on Nasdaq. Independent public company (~$20B+ market cap; ~$3B TTM revenue as of FY2025/26).
- Leading independent identity provider. Workforce Identity Cloud (SSO, MFA, lifecycle, IGA, PAM, device access) + Customer Identity Cloud (Auth0, acquired 2021 for ~$6.5B).
