# Semgrep / r2c (search synthesis)

- Company legal/early name: r2c; Crunchbase entry under "r2c". Rebranded to Semgrep.
- Founded 2017 by Drew Dennison, Isaac Evans, Luke O'Malley; HQ San Francisco.
- Series D: $100M, February 2025, led by Menlo Ventures; total funding to ~$193M as of March 2025.
- Open-source Semgrep OSS released end of 2020; commercial platform = Semgrep Code (SAST), Supply Chain (SCA), Secrets.
