GovernGPT — "The RFP Platform Asset Managers Adopt"

What it does: Automates filling out due-diligence questionnaires (DDQs) and RFPs for
asset managers. AI finds fresh answers, maintains content databases, and facilitates
intracompany communication/review — completing in minutes what previously took teams
days to do manually. Exports filled-out forms in their original format.

Founded: 2023
Founders: Mamal Amini (ML; previously worked with Yoshua Bengio), Oliver Walerys
(product/software; self-taught developer).
HQ: Montreal, Canada (note: other sources say Toronto — both Canada).
YC batch: Winter 2024 (W24).
Team size: ~6 people.
Target customers: Asset managers raising capital — hedge funds, private equity,
private credit. Named clients: Bridgewater, Pantheon, Coatue, Onex.

Function is clearly DDQ/RFP-response automation (back-office IR/fundraising workflow),
NOT AI model-risk governance.
