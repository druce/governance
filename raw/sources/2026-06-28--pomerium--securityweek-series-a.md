- Funding: $13.75 million Series A, announced June 20, 2024.
- Investors: Benchmark (lead), Bain Capital, Haystack, SNR, and angels.
- Total raised to date: ~$18 million.
- Founded: 2019. Founder/CEO: Bobby DeSimone.
- HQ: California.
- What it does: zero trust reverse proxy that centralizes authentication and authorization for secure access to apps/services; continuous identity verification across logins, behaviors, devices, locations; gateway proxies traffic and keeps assets off direct user exposure; treats access requests individually (per-request) rather than connection-based.
