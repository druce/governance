Good for: Confirming Ping Identity ownership (Thoma Bravo PE) and the ForgeRock merger into Ping.

Key facts:
- Thoma Bravo completed acquisition of ForgeRock (~$2.3B, agreed Oct 2022) and combined ForgeRock into its portfolio company Ping Identity. Completion announced August 2023, after DOJ approval.
- Thoma Bravo had taken Ping Identity private earlier — agreed Aug 2022 for ~$2.8B (Ping had been public on NYSE under ticker PING since 2019 IPO).
- Combined company led by Ping founder/CEO Andre Durand.
- Ping Identity = private equity owned (Thoma Bravo). Identity & Access Management (IAM): workforce + customer identity (CIAM), SSO, MFA, access management, directory. HQ Denver, Colorado; founded 2002.

Corroboration: Reuters/Dark Reading/Biometric Update covered the merger Aug 2023.
