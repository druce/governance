## Key facts
- Founded 2016 by Dimitri Sirota (CEO) and Nimrod Vax. Veterans of identity, data security, big data, governance.
- HQ: New York, NY; additional offices in US and Israel.
- What it does: Enterprise Data Security Platform combining DSPM, DLP, data access governance (DAG), data privacy, and AI governance/AI security in one unified platform. Known for deep ML-based data discovery and classification at scale (correlation/cluster-based PII discovery), heavily used for privacy (GDPR/CCPA) and data governance, now extended to AI data security.
- Funding: closed a $60M growth round bringing total capital raised to ~$320M at a valuation over $1 billion. Growth round led by Riverwood Capital, with Silver Lake Waterman and Advent participating. (Earlier major backers across rounds included Bessemer, Boldstart, Comcast Ventures, Salesforce Ventures, Tiger Global, Glynn Capital, etc.)
- M&A: independent; no acquisition. No seed M&A flag.
- Target customer: large enterprise, Fortune 500, regulated industries.
