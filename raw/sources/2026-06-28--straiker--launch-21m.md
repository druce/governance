## Key extracted facts
- Founded 2024; emerged from stealth March 27, 2025. HQ Sunnyvale, CA.
- Founders: Ankur Shah (Co-founder & CEO; ex-Palo Alto Networks, led Prisma Cloud), Sreenath Kurupati (Co-founder; ex-Palo Alto / Intel).
- Funding: $21M Series A (March 2025) from Lightspeed Venture Partners and Bain Capital Ventures.
- Product (AI-native security platform), three modules:
  - Ascend AI — automated attack simulation / red teaming with AI-specific safety & security scenarios; continuous testing.
  - Defend AI — runtime protection, real-time threat detection and automated blocking across layers of AI apps.
  - Discover AI — AI agent discovery, tool monitoring, governance visibility at scale.
- Uses fine-tuned models analyzing the full AI stack (user, model, application, agents, identity, data).
- Target: enterprises deploying AI apps/agents across finance, healthcare, technology, media, retail.
