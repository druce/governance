# Semgrep (Contrary Research extract)

- Founded: 2017 (originally as r2c). Semgrep OSS released end of 2020.
- Founders: Isaac Evans (CEO), Drew Dennison (CTO), Luke O'Malley (CPO)
- HQ: San Francisco, CA
- Ownership: Private (independent; VC-backed)
- Total funding: $193M
- Series D: $100M, February 2025, led by Menlo Ventures (existing investors Felicis, Harpoon, Lightspeed, Redpoint, Sequoia participated)
- Product:
  - SAST (Semgrep Code) — fast static analysis, custom rules, AI false-positive reduction
  - Secrets (Semgrep Secrets) — API keys / password leak detection
  - Supply Chain (Semgrep Supply Chain / SCA) — third-party dependency vuln scanning, reachability to cut false positives
- Deployment/pricing: SaaS subscription per contributor/month (~$40 Code & Supply Chain, ~$20 Secrets); free tier under 10 contributors. Also open-source Semgrep OSS engine (CLI).
