## Key extracted facts
- Founded 2018. HQ: New York City (140 Crosby Street).
- Independent (not acquired). Funding ~$63M total. $3.3M seed (launch); $42M Series B in Sept 2022 led by Acrew Capital and Greycroft, with Index Ventures, Work-Bench. Other investors: Index Ventures, Work-Bench, Acrew.
- Product evolution: began as ML model monitoring / performance + explainability + bias detection ("performance, bias, explainability" across tabular, CV, NLP, LLM). Now positions as "AI governance for everyone" / control plane to "ship reliable AI agents fast."
- Current capabilities: continuous evals across the AI lifecycle; Agent Discovery & Governance (discover agents, enforce policy, oversight); built-in guardrails (firewall) against misuse / off-brand interactions; model-agnostic (traditional ML, GenAI, agentic).
- Deployment: SaaS, on-premises, or via GCP / AWS marketplace. Strong on-prem / self-hosted story (regulated buyers).
- Open source: Arthur Engine (github arthur-ai/arthur-engine); previously Arthur Bench (LLM eval benchmark, OSS).
- Customers: Axios, Upsolve, Expel; historically banks/financial firms and government for ML monitoring.
