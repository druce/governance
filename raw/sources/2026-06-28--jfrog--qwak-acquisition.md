Key extracted text:
- Announcement date: June 25, 2024.
- JFrog Ltd. — Nasdaq: FROG. HQ: Sunnyvale, California (dual HQ with Netanya, Israel).
- JFrog to acquire Qwak AI Ltd., creators of an AI/MLOps platform (model training, serving, deployment).
- Deal value not disclosed in the release; press reports (Globes, Calcalist) cite ~$230 million.
- Artifactory serves as the model registry / single system of record, storing ML models as packages
  like any software component.
- Xray provides security scanning of ML models for compliance/security.
- Goal: unified system of record across Dev, Sec, ML and Ops (DevOps + DevSecOps + MLOps + MLSecOps).
- Qwak founded 2020 (Alon Lev, Lior Penso, Ran Romano, Yuval Fernbach); ~45 employees; ~$27M raised
  prior (Bessemer, Leaders Fund, StageOne, Nordstar, Amiti).
