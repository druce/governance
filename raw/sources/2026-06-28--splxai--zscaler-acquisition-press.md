Announcement date: November 3, 2025.
Zscaler (NASDAQ: ZS) announced it acquired AI security pioneer SPLX. Terms not disclosed in the release.
SPLX specializes in: AI asset discovery, automated red teaming (5,000+ attack simulations), threat
inspection, runtime guardrails, prompt hardening, and governance across the AI lifecycle.
Acquisition extends the Zscaler Zero Trust Exchange with AI asset discovery, automated red teaming,
runtime guardrails, and governance; integrates SPLX tech for discovery, risk assessment, and remediation
to help enterprises secure models, agent workflows, and RAG pipelines from development through deployment.

Quotes:
- Jay Chaudhry (Zscaler CEO): "AI is creating enormous value, but its full potential can only be realized
  when it can be secured."
- Kristian Kamber (SPLX CEO & co-founder): "By joining forces, we'll bring our innovation to one of the
  most trusted security platforms in the world."

CLOSE STATUS (corroborated separately): Zscaler closed the acquisitions of Red Canary Inc. and SPLXAI Inc.
during Q1 fiscal 2026 for an aggregate purchase price of $692.0M (combined, not separately disclosed; Red
Canary was the much larger component). Per Zscaler Form 8-K / FY2026 Q1 disclosure.
