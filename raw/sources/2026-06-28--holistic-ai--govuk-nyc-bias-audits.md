Note: UK Government (DSIT) AI assurance techniques portfolio case study on Holistic AI's NYC bias audit. Independent, primary-government source; good for the bias-audit / NYC Local Law 144 capability and the assurance-technique categorization.

Key extracted text:
- Holistic AI's platform conducts independent audits of automated employment decision tools (AEDTs) used in hiring and promotion decisions.
- "calculates the required metrics to compare the output of the tool for different subgroups based on their protected characteristics such as race/ethnicity and sex/gender."
- Assesses systems at multiple levels; delivers findings via dashboard; produces a public Summary of Results plus internal reports.
- Directly addresses NYC Local Law 144 (bias audits for AEDTs): helps employers publish transparency summaries, provide candidate notices, calculate fairness impact ratios across protected groups.
- Assurance technique category: Bias Audit and Impact Evaluation (technical + procedural). Sector: Administrative & Support Services; function: Human Resources.
- Maps to principles: Transparency & Explainability; Fairness (impact ratios); Accountability & Governance.
