## Key facts (as of 2026-06-30)

- **Category:** AI-native Security Operations / agentic SOC platform (next-gen SIEM-replacement). "AI for security," not "security for AI." It defends an enterprise's existing infra with AI agents; does not govern LLM usage / shadow-AI / model egress.
- **What it does:** AI-native threat detection, investigation, and response. AI agents autonomously hunt threats, generate per-customer detections, investigate every signal, and assemble "attack stories." Correlates across identity, cloud, endpoint, network, SaaS.
- **Architecture:** proprietary "dynamic data model" built per-customer from their telemetry; **federated queries** retrieve data on-demand from the customer's own cloud storage / data lakes / SIEMs rather than ingesting everything (pitched ~1/5 the cost of ingest-priced SIEMs). SaaS overlay, no rip-and-replace.
- **Integrations (vendor site):** Okta, Ping, Entra (identity); AWS, GCP, Azure (cloud); Snowflake, Databricks, S3, Cribl (data); Splunk, Elastic (SIEM); ServiceNow, Jira (case mgmt). Automated response e.g. identity isolation.
- **Funding:** Emerged from stealth 2026-04-15 with $70M total — $15M seed + $55M Series A (Series A led by Felicis). Other investors: First Round Capital, Brightmind Partners; Calcalist also lists Theory VC, Two Sigma, Lockstep + angels from Demisto, Abnormal, Splunk, CrowdStrike, Palo Alto, Microsoft, Okta. Valuation not disclosed.
- **Founded:** ~late 2025 ("six-month-old" as of April 2026). HQ: New York City; Israeli-founded team.
- **Founders:** Shachar Hirshberg (CEO) — ex-Demisto (acq. by Palo Alto ~$600M), led AWS GuardDuty, Harvard MBA. Dan Shiebler (CTO) — led AI/ML at Abnormal AI, PhD ML, Oxford.
- **Headcount:** ~30, expanding toward ~65 by end of 2026 (single source, Calcalist).
- **Customers (vendor logos, unverified):** Mercury, Wix, Lemonade, Upwork, Amazon Security, Sony. Target sectors: technology, banking, financial services.
- **Claimed metrics (vendor marketing, unverified):** "96% reduction in MTTR" (to <5 min); "2B+ events/hour"; "15,000+ TB daily."
- **M&A:** none — too early.

## Caveats
- Customer logos and headline metrics are vendor-sourced / unverified. Granular funding split + extended investor list come mainly from one source (Calcalist). Exact incorporation date and any Israel R&D center not confirmed.
