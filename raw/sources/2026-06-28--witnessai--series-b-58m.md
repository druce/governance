## Key extracted facts
- **$58M Series B** announced **2026-01-13**, led by **Sound Ventures** (early OpenAI/Anthropic/SentinelOne investor), with Fin Capital, Qualcomm Ventures, Samsung Ventures, Forgepoint Capital.
- Prior: **$27.5M Series A** (May 2024), led by **GV (Google Ventures)** and **Ballistic Ventures** (which incubated WitnessAI). **Total to date: $86.5M.**
- Ownership: **independent** (no acquisition; raising growth capital).
- Founded **2023** by **Rick Caccia (co-founder & CEO)** and **Gil Spencer**. HQ **Mountain View, California**.
- Product: AI enablement / security-governance platform for enterprise AI usage. Modules: Observe (monitor/audit AI activity & risk, app visibility), Govern (access governance — identity-aware policy on who can use which AI for what), Protect (runtime — block prompt injection, multi-turn attacks, data misuse). New **Agentic Control**: secures AI-agent activity like LLM activity, monitoring which agents/MCP servers/tools are active and what an agent does on a user's behalf.
- Deployment: "enterprise-first, single-tenant architecture" for data sovereignty/compliance; hybrid cloud + edge; operates inline on AI traffic (proxy-style observability + enforcement).
- Traction (marketing): 500%+ ARR growth, 5x headcount in trailing 12 months.
