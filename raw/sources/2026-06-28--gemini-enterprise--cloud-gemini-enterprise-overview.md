Key extracted text:

- On/around 2025-10-09, Google Cloud rebranded "Google Agentspace" into "Gemini Enterprise." The Agentspace URL now redirects to the Gemini Enterprise page. Existing Agentspace customers continue with no disruption to features/billing/support.
- Google Agentspace will no longer be available for NEW subscriptions starting 2025-12-31.
- Gemini Enterprise is "powered by the same conversational and orchestration technology from Agentspace," now enhanced with Google DeepMind models and built-in Google agents — "a unified, secure platform that lets every employee search, analyze, generate, and automate within their existing tools."
- Ships a library of ready-to-use agents: Deep Research, NotebookLM, Coding Agents.
- Marketed as the "front door" to Google AI for the enterprise: enterprise search/grounding across company data + agent build/run.
- Editions: Gemini Enterprise Standard, Gemini Enterprise Plus (plus NotebookLM Enterprise as related SKU).
- Standard/Plus support: data residency (region of choice), Google Cloud audit logging (immutable logs), and Customer Managed Encryption Keys (CMEK) via Cloud KMS.
