Pinecone is an independent, VC-backed managed vector database company.
- Founded: 2019 by Edo Liberty (ex-AWS SageMaker, ex-Yahoo Research).
- HQ: New York City (offices in San Francisco and Tel Aviv).
- Series B: $100M led by Andreessen Horowitz (a16z), with ICONIQ Growth, Menlo
  Ventures, Wing Venture Capital (2023). Post-money valuation ~$750M.
- Total raised: ~$138M across seed/A/B.
- Product: fully-managed, serverless vector database (SaaS) for similarity search /
  RAG long-term memory for AI apps. Still independent as of 2026-06.
