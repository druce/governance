# Palo Alto Networks Completes Acquisition of Portkey to Secure AI Agents

URL: https://www.paloaltonetworks.com/company/press/2026/palo-alto-networks-completes-acquisition-of-portkey-to-secure-ai-agents
Fetched: 2026-06-28
Good for: Primary confirmation of PANW–Portkey deal + how Portkey folds into Prisma AIRS.

## Key facts
- Announcement of intent: April 30, 2026 (PANW press release "to Acquire Portkey").
- Completion date: May 29, 2026.
- Deal terms: NOT disclosed.
- What Portkey is: AI Gateway pioneer; control plane for monitoring, orchestrating, governing autonomous AI agents at enterprise scale; processes trillions of tokens/month with low latency for agent-to-agent comms.
- Integration: Portkey becomes the foundational AI Gateway component of Prisma AIRS, enabling:
  - AI Runtime Security (inspecting all AI traffic for agent-based threats)
  - Agent Identity Security (authenticating agentic interactions, preventing unauthorized tool use)
  - AI Observability (telemetry for production-scale performance)
- Quotes: Lee Klarich (Chief Product & Technology Officer); Rohit Agarwal (Portkey CEO/co-founder).

## Company background (corroborating press)
- Founded 2023 by Rohit Agarwal and Ayush Garg; HQ San Francisco, CA.
- Seed: ~$3M (Lightspeed, Aug 2023). Series A: $15M led by Elevation Capital w/ Lightspeed, announced 2026-02-19 — i.e., raised Series A only ~3 months before PANW acquisition.
