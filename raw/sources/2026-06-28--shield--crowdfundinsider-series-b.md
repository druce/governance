Crowdfund Insider — "Regtech Shield Raises $20 Million Series B, OurCrowd Joins Round"
(2022-12)

- Israel-based regtech; $20M Series B.
- Led by Macquarie Capital; joined by UBS Next and Mindset; OurCrowd backed (also
  participated in Series A).
- Communication & compliance platform; leverages AI to mitigate risks including
  financial crime, privacy, and misconduct.
- Series A ($15M) closed early 2022; ~$35M total raised in 2022. Funds to expand in
  existing markets and into US.
