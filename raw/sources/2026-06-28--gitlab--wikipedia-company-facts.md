## Key extracted text

- Founded 2014 by Dmytro Zaporozhets (Ukrainian) and Sytse "Sid" Sijbrandij (Dutch); builds on an open-source project started 2011.
- HQ: San Francisco; operates as an all-remote company (~2,375 employees across 60+ countries as of 2025).
- IPO: October 14, 2021 on NASDAQ under ticker "GTLB" at ~$10B valuation.
- Ownership: remains independent, publicly traded. In July 2024 Reuters reported GitLab was exploring a potential sale after attracting acquisition interest, with Datadog mentioned as an interested party.
- 2018: considered the first partly-Ukrainian unicorn.
